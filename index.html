ABOUT ME

a little about myself

My journey into design started with a curiosity for how people interact with technology. After completing my MCA from MIT Pune, I moved through enterprise work at Infosys, fintech at PayU, and now into the scale of Jio Platforms — one of India's largest tech ecosystems.

I'm drawn to problems where good design genuinely changes outcomes: where a clearer flow reduces support calls, a systematic approach multiplies a team's output, or an AI interaction earns real user trust. RISD, IxDF, and IBM certified. 9+ years in.
